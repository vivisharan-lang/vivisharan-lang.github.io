Ericsson — Mechanical Developer

I am currently working on 6G testbeds, where I contribute to the development of test systems that enable continuous integration and validation of emerging technologies.

A testbed is a controlled experimental environment where new technologies, systems, and concepts can be developed, tested, and evaluated before being deployed in real-world applications.

In my role as a mechanical developer, I collaborate with multiple cross-functional teams to support the design and development of these testbeds, ensuring they meet both technical and operational requirements.

In addition to my technical work, I am part of the core team of GenNext at Ericsson, which focuses on fostering inclusion, diversity, and a sense of belonging for early-career professionals.

As part of this initiative, we organize social and learning events to engage our community and create a supportive environment for networking, knowledge sharing, and professional growth.
